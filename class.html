Whate is BMI?

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on an individual's weight and height. It is used to determine if a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. A BMI of 18.5 to 24.9 is considered to be in the normal range, while a BMI of 25 or above is considered to be overweight or obese.
